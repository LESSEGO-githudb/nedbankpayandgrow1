About Pay and Grow

Nedbank Kasi Pay & Grow empowers informal township entrepreneurs by eliminating the barrier of traditional financial gatekeeping. Our data-free smart terminal lets you accept digital card payments with zero monthly rental fees, while our unique AI credit facility tracks your daily transactions to automatically unlock stock advances and affordable loans, finally providing the safe, professional financial partner your business deserves.

Our Mission

We strive to replace traditional, rigid banking gatekeeping with a partner that respects your hustle, offering secure card payment solutions and stock funding based on your daily trading performance rather than formal paperwork.
